Welcome to the Forbidden Lands Companion App!

The purpose of this app is to serve as an assistant when playing the role playing game Forbidden Lands by Fria Ligan.

Features

  • Creating new characters from scratch
  • Creating characters from pre-made templates
  • Inventory management
  • Experience and Reputation management
  • Language support for English, Swedish and German
  • A dice roller, of course :)
